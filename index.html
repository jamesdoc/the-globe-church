We are a church made up of many stories. But we are seeking to understand the One Story. The greatest story. A story of love, of treason, of redemption, of joy. The story of God and His World.

A story to hear - We believe that God speaks as the Bible is read and preached. So we are committed to enabling as many people as possible to engage with the voice of the Living God.

A story to enjoy - We believe that eternal joy is found in knowing Jesus. So we are committed to knowing and experiencing that joy together - even in our suffering.

A story to share - We believe that God is deeply generous. So we are committed to reflecting that generosity. We long to be a church where people are transformed, equipped and then sent out to serve Jesus all over the world.
